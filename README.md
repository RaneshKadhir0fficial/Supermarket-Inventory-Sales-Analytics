# 📖 Overview

Modern supermarkets generate thousands of transactions every day through Point-of-Sale (POS) systems. While this data contains valuable business insights, raw transactional records alone cannot support strategic decision-making.

This project presents a **Supermarket Inventory & Sales Analytics Platform** that integrates inventory management, supplier monitoring, sales analytics, and automated reorder management into a single decision-support system.

Inspired by modern Business Intelligence workflows, the platform demonstrates how operational data can be transformed into meaningful business insights for inventory optimization and sales performance analysis. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Retail businesses often face challenges such as:

- Overstocking increases inventory holding costs.
- Understocking leads to lost sales opportunities.
- Manual inventory tracking is inefficient.
- Supplier delays affect product availability.
- Business decisions are often made without analytical insights.

An integrated analytics platform is required to monitor inventory, analyze sales, optimize supplier performance, and improve operational efficiency. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
